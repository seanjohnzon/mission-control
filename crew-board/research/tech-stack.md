## Thousand Sunny Design References

### Sanji’s Kitchen
Located on the first floor, fore part of the Thousand Sunny, this is a functional and stylish space for cooking and dining. Equipped with a large oven, a locked refrigerator (to deter Luffy), and ample counter space, it reflects Sanji’s meticulous nature with organized storage and a polished look.

### Chopper’s Sick Bay
Situated on the first floor near the rear, this compact medical room includes a patient bed, shelves of supplies, and Chopper’s desk for research. It has a cozy, reassuring vibe with personal touches like Chopper’s notes, essential for treating the crew.

### Robin’s Library
On the second floor, this archaeological research room is a quiet haven with books, maps, and documents for studying Poneglyphs. Featuring a large desk and shelves, it’s a dimly lit, serene space reflecting Robin’s intellectual pursuits.
