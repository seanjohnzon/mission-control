👥 Department Architecture
Captain sets intent & approvals → Nami routes work → Department Chiefs break down missions & spawn subagents → Execution + QA gates → Vivi/Jinbe keep threads & board clean.

🧠 MC Architecture Reflection

Mission Control isn’t a single “agent” — it’s a layered org chart: human oversight at the top, routing in the middle, specialized departments beneath, and repeatable process at the bottom. The goal is fast parallel execution without losing quality gates or human judgment.
👤 CaptainDirection
Defines constraints, priorities, and the definition of “done”. Final approval always stays human.
🧭 NamiRouting
Turns intent into assignable missions, selects departments, tracks budget/time, and prevents thrash.
ChiefsDecomposition
Engineering (Franky), Automation (Sanji), QA (Chopper), BI/Specs (Usopp), Research (Robin), Creative/UX (Brook), Thread Mgmt (Vivi), Scrum (Jinbe).
SubagentsParallelism
Small scoped executors (build, fetch, test, report). Spawned with clear inputs/outputs so work stays traceable.
