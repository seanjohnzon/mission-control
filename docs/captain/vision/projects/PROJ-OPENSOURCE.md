# PROJ-OPENSOURCE — Go Open Source & Go Viral

## Connection to Vision
This is the FIRST major project on the road to Laugh Tale. Without open-sourcing, the power stays with us. The whole point is to give it to everyone.

## What
Turn the Straw Hat crew into an open-source OpenClaw template that anyone can install and become their own Captain.

## Why
- Give people Devil Fruit powers before corporations lock AI down
- Build viral fanbase through One Piece community
- Create the foundation for PROJ-GAME and PROJ-CHARACTERS
- Every user who installs becomes a content creator (streaming potential)

## Target Audience
Gen Z who grew up with One Piece. They already understand:
- Crew dynamics (Luffy's magnetic leadership)
- Devil Fruit powers (special abilities)
- The Grand Line journey (pursuing dreams against all odds)
- Nakama (crew bonds that make you stronger)

## Prerequisites
- Stable crew system (departments operational)
- Mission Control polished (the user's dashboard)
- Hiring process automated (so users can hire their own crew)
- Documentation complete (users need to self-onboard)

## Success Metrics
- GitHub stars
- Community forks and customizations
- Streaming content featuring the crew
- One Piece fan community engagement

## Status
Planning. Current focus: get our crew operational first.
